Target Audience

The target audience for this website will be citizens of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, as well as possible visitors to these areas. The site will be geared towards both male and femals between the ages of 15 and 35 with a high school education. Visitors to the site may use a small mobile device and a tablet sized device or larger.
