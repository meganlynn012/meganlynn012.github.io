Tour the Island

Wondering where to take your scooter on the island? Are you unsure of what to explore beyond the paved roads of Cozumel? Check out what ~Scoots has below to help.

We recommend starting your adventure off with a tour to become more familiar with the island. You can also bundle your tour with a rental to get incredible deals!

Scooter Sightseeing

Come check out the island, the amazing beaches, beautiful harbors, and end your tour at Cozumel's hottest shopping district. This is perfect for you if this is your firt time to the island to get a feel for what Cozumel has to offer. You'll wade in crystal clear ocean water, feel the ocean breeze on your face as you ride, and learn how to navigate the city. If you're lucky, you'll meet some baby turtles! Best experienced with the Honda Dio or Honda PCX-150.

Tour Length: 2 Hours
Price per Adult: $40.00
Start Times: 8:00 ~ 11:00 ~ 13:00 ~ 16:00
Book your Scooter and Tour

Cozumel Food Tour

Join us for Cozumel's ultimate dining experience. Peruse downtown San Miguel with frequent taste breaks at the locals' favorite restaraunts. You'll try cuisines from places such as Los O Tates, El Morro, and El Pique (taco pizza!). This tour is a perfect addition to the Honda Metropolitan for a laid back ride through the city. Ticket prices include the cost of your meals.

Tour Length: 3 Hours
Price per Adult: $80.00
Start Times: 10:00 ~ 12:00 ~ 14:00
Book your Scooter and Tour

Off-roading to Mayan Ruins

Need a little more adventure in your itineray (with a touch of education if you're into that)? Join us for an off road excursion through central Cozumel to see the lost cities of this civilization that date back two millenium! You'll explore Chichen Itza, the fortress of Tulum and the Mayan Jungle. Scooters won't cut it for this trip. You'll need the Honda Pioneer, or a Jeep Wrangler.

Tour Length: 4 Hours
Price per Adult: $60.00
Start Times: 8:00 ~ 13:00
