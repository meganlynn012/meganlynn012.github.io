A Piece of Olaf

I surprisingly loved Olaf's Frozen Adventure. I put it on in an effort to entertain my 4-year old for the 10 or so minutes it lasts and found I wasn't able to stop watching it myself. I'm not a fun person to watch movies with if I don't particularly like the storyline. This is often how I am with most comedies containing crude humor. I pick up on all the little bits the filmakers leave out or don't explain and exploit them to everyone who will listen. I actually did like Frozen so I didn't bring up as many questions, but I always wondered, how did Olaf know his name and why does he like warm hugs? Why does he have a dream to experience summer? Disney's Olaf's Frozen Adventure answered all those questions and more for me. It was actually hilarious, and I love seeing stories from a different perspective.
