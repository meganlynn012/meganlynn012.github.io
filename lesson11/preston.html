Perfect Strawberry Limeade

No summer picnic is complete with the perfect blended, iced, strawberry flavored lemonade. This recipe is derived from a fellow Idahoean found on Our Best Bites blog. You'll need lots of ice, some Sprite, a couple of limes, and 3 tbsps (or 4 if you really like strawberries) of strawberry sauce. Oh, and a glass. Fill a glass with ice then squeeze the juice of one lime into the class. Fill the glass halfway with Sprite and top with strawberry sauce. Fill the glass the rest of the way with Sprite. Served best cold, and with a metal straw. The straw absorbs the chill from the ice, making the drink even colder for those hot summer days.
