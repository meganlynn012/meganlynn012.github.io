The Leprechaun Chase

Have you ever attempted to reach the end of the rainbow? It not, it's always been tempting, right? A pot of gold lies at the end of the rainbow. But what actually happens if you reach the end of the rainbow (assuming it is the correct end)? Impossible some may say, but it happened to me. I reached the end of the rainbow and even glimpsed the pot of gold. I was so shocked the legends were true, I had frozen in place. Consequently, the gold belong to the smallest creature I had ever laid eyes on. He had a red beard, a green jacket, and a buckle on his hat, his best, and on each shoe. He jumped out from behind a tree trunk and with impossible strength, picked up that pot of cold and sped off quicker than a jack rabbit.
